PRE EDH is a homebrew format cooked by my friend group. There are two distinct philosophies at play: the first is that EDH got worse after Wizards started officially acknowledging the format in their game design, therefore every card that references a commander, the command zone, color identities etc. is banned here. The second is that UB is immersion breaking, therefore all UB cards are banned regardless of their mechanics.
